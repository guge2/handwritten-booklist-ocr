Let us spend one day as deliberately as Nature,
and not be thrown off the track by every nutshell
and mosquito's wing that falls on the rails; let us
rise early and fast, or break fast, gently and
without perturbation; let company come and let
company go; let the bells ring and the children
cry, determined to make a day of it.
